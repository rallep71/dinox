Donations & transparency

Support, payment processing, and notes

What this is about

DinoX is an open-source project. If you want to support development, you can make a voluntary donation. Donations are voluntary contributions and do not include any entitlement to support, features, or other consideration.

Ways to donate

  • Ko-fi: ko-fi.com/rallep
  • GitHub Sponsors: github.com/sponsors/rallep71

Payment processing / personal data

Payment processing is handled by the respective platforms (Ko-fi and GitHub). Personal data (e.g. name, payment data, email address) may be processed by the platform provider under their terms. DinoX does not process payment data directly.
